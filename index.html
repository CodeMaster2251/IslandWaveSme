About Us

We are a local SME residing in Port Moresby in Papua New Guinea. We sell locally made meri blouses, mens' shirts and other items. The design of clothing and item we offer is purpousfully to meet the favouritism of todays dressing style and to make such admirable clothing eccessible to the people here in PNG.

The type of sales we perform is Cash on delivery.All the available items we sell can be ordered online and negotiaton can be done inorder to arrange for the delivery.

How we started

We started with a simple sales of Meri blouse in our front yard two years ago. As we grow in our SME, we observe that people seems to like local designs and styles of clothing that are up to date. Especcially when it comes to design, many people like one design or designs that are favoured by many. Therefore we came up with an idea to make our SME online inorder to sell specific design of clothing and other items that is favoured by most people. That leads us to create a website that can attract more customers by offering up to date designs of clothing so we can satisfy our custormers and also we can improve in our SME strategy.

Contact Us
