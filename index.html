About Us

We are a local SME residing in Port Moresby in Papua New Guinea. Our local SME aims at selling Pacific trending clothing that is admired by many Pacific Island countries. The design of clothing and item we offer is purpousfully to meet the favouritism of todays dressing style and to make such admirable clothing eccessible to the people here in PNG.

The type of sales we perform is of monthly period.All the available items we sell are for only 1 month and the next month, we'll come up with different design sales.

How we started

We started with a simple sales of Meri blouse in our front yard two years ago. As we grow in our SME, we observe that people in PNG seems to like styles of clothing that are up to date. Especcially when it comes to design, many people like one design or one type of clothing within a short period of time and then they seems to dislike the design again. We noticed that sift in favouritism so we came up with an idea to make our SME online inorder to meet their hunger of favouritism for a specific design. That leads us to create a website that can attract more customers by offering up to date designs of clothing within a month so that we can satisfy our custormers and also we can improve in our SME strategy.
